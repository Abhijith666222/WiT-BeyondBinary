# SignBridge Universe — Demo script (2 min)

## For judges / evaluators

1. **Landing (15 s)**  
   - Open http://localhost:3000  
   - Show hero: “SignBridge Universe”, “Start Demo”, “Go to Universe Home”  
   - Point to persona cards: Deaf, Blind, Deaf-blind, Helper  

2. **Onboarding (20 s)**  
   - Click **Start Demo**  
   - Select **Deaf user** (camera-first)  
   - Click **Continue to Universe Home**  

3. **Dashboard (15 s)**  
   - Show “Universe Home” and the four modules  
   - Optional: click **Start demo tour** for guided overlay  

4. **SignWave (30 s)**  
   - Click **SignWave**  
   - Pick a quick phrase (e.g. “Hello”) or an SgSL sign (e.g. THANK)  
   - Show: text output, “Speak again” (TTS), sign gloss, haptic indicator  
   - Optional: **Start mic**, speak, **Stop mic** → same outputs  

5. **TouchSpeak (20 s)**  
   - Click **TouchSpeak**  
   - **Receive**: type “Hi”, click **Play braille + vibrate** (cells animate; on mobile, vibrates)  
   - **Send**: tap dots to form a letter, **Add letter**, **Speak**  
   - Tap a quick response phrase  

6. **ContextAI (20 s)**  
   - Click **ContextAI** → **Shopping assist**  
   - Click **Run OCR (mock)** → wait for results  
   - Tap speaker on a result line (TTS)  
   - Optional: Hawker (menu OCR mock), MRT (arrivals + haptic turn cues)  

7. **Learning (20 s)**  
   - Click **Learning**  
   - **Classroom**: click **Enter classroom** → Start mic, speak → see subtitles + gloss  
   - **Sign Quest**: click **Play** → answer quiz (match sign to meaning), see score/streak  

8. **Simulator & Connectors (15 s)**  
   - **Simulator**: select a persona to preview, **Apply** to switch  
   - **Connectors**: Zoom (mock join), LTA (mock arrivals), Gov (SingPass mock modal)  

9. **Pitch (30 s)**  
   - Open **/pitch** for problem + solution  

## Wow moment

- One mesh: sign/speech/text in → text, TTS, sign gloss, haptic out.  
- Persona changes dashboard copy and primary output (Deaf vs Blind vs Deaf-blind).  
- TouchSpeak: real braille cell + vibration (mobile) and quick phrases.  
- ContextAI and Learning are clickable with mock OCR and quiz.
